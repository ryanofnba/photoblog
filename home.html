Blog

Hard Work

Sept 20th, 2016

People always say that hard work will eventually pay off. Is that true? To me it is. If you're working hard to became a billionaire, or to became the most popular person, then that saying may not be always true. If you work hard with the mindset to improve yourself and make the people around you happier, then that saying is definitely true.

"Pay off" isn't always tangible. The intangibles will make you much more satisfied with the work you put in.

Adulthood / Childhood

Sept 20th, 2016

Remember when you were 6, 7, 8 years old? When everything was as simple as getting to play with the toy that we longed for? When smiling was actually a sincere reflection of how you felt?

Now, try to remember when you began to lose that purity. When the world became an arena where you have to fight to survive. When you first started to put on a mask to cover your true feelings.

Now think again, has the world really changed that much since you were a kid? The world is simple, we just became more complicated.

Siblings

Sept 20th, 2016

Many of us grew up with siblings. Sometimes we hate them. Maybe it's jealousy, maybe it's competition, maybe it's family problems.

At the end of the day, we still love them more than we hate them. Simply because they are our brothers and sisters.
